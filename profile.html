SIAMAYA Chocolate Company Profile


Overview

Founded in 2017 and headquartered in Chiang Mai, Thailand, SIAMAYA Chocolate is a leading manufacturer of bean-to-bar cocoa products and is renowned for its innovative approach to fine artisanal chocolate. The company was founded by passionate food enthusiast Neil Ransom in 2017 and has been transforming locally-sourced Thai cocoa beans into award-winning chocolates, infusing them with unique Thai flavors for almost a decade now. SIAMAYA Chocolate is committed to quality and sustainability, showcasing the rich traditional flavors of Thailand through its handcrafted products.

Mission and Vision

SIAMAYA Chocolate's mission is to create exceptional chocolate products that highlight the rich flavors and natural ingredients of Thailand while adhering to the highest standards of craftsmanship. The company is committed to becoming a global leader in the artisanal chocolate industry, driving sustainable practices through education and collaboration, and fostering community engagement.

Products and Innovation

SIAMAYA Chocolate focuses on handcrafting chocolates using premium Thai cocoa beans and local ingredients. The company is known for its unique flavor combinations, perfectly blending traditional Thai flavors such as Thai milk tea, coconut, chili, tom yum soup, and even durian with high-quality chocolate. These also include seasonal and regional limited editions.

SIAMAYA also offers vegan options and interactive experiences such as chocolate-making workshops and truffle events, thus allowing customers to create their own personalized chocolates. The company's commitment to innovation has earned it numerous international chocolate awards, further solidifying its outstanding reputation in the industry.

Sustainability and Community Engagement

SIAMAYA Chocolate Company adheres to a sustainable development philosophy, sourcing cocoa beans directly from local Thai farmers to ensure fair trade and a supply of high-quality raw materials. The company works closely with local organizations, including schools and charities (such as Zoe, which supports street children), to promote education and social impact. SIAMAYA's packaging designs, created by local artists and inspired by the vibrant colors of Lanna fabrics, fully reflect the company's support for the Chiang Mai community and its promotion of Northern Thai culture.

Operations and Market Coverage

SIAMAYA is headquartered in Chiang Mai, with its factory located near Wat Phra That Doi Suthep, where all products are meticulously crafted. The company's products are available at its flagship store near Tha Pae Gate, as well as at select retail outlets throughout Thailand, including Rimping, Gourmet Markets, Big C, Central Foodhall, Baimiang, and Dear Tummy at Icon Siam.

Furthermore, SIAMAYA conducts international sales through select partners, such as Marriott, Anantara and Mövenpick and already ships to China (exclusive distributor), Japan, the United States, Australia and Europe.

Customer Experience

SIAMAYA chocolate offers visitors unique interactive experiences, including factory tours, chocolate tastings, and workshops suitable for adults and children (minimum age 7 years old, accompanied by an adult). These activities provide in-depth demonstrations of the complete chocolate-making process from cocoa beans to finished chocolates, allowing participants to explore the art of chocolate making. Customer reviews particularly highlight the attractive, high-quality packaging and unique, innovative flavors with our Durian, Thai Tea, Coconut Curry, Tom Yum, Thai Bullet Chili and Mango Chili flavors being particularly popular among Chinese customers.

Leadership and Values

Under the leadership of founder Neil Ransom, SIAMAYA Chocolate combines the passion for Asian cuisine with a focused dedication to chocolate craftsmanship. The founder learned the chocolate-making process from scratch and is committed to building community connections, reflecting the company's core values ​​of quality, creativity, and social responsibility.

Recognition and Future Vision

SIAMAYA Chocolate has gained widespread international acclaim and numerous chocolate industry awards for its innovative flavors and unwavering pursuit of quality. The company continues to expand its product line, explore new flavor combinations, and strengthen its brand influence in both local and international markets. Upholding a commitment to sustainability and community engagement, SIAMAYA is steadily progressing towards becoming a leading brand in the fine chocolate industry.

SIAMAYA Chocolate cordially invites chocolate lovers and curious visitors to experience the essence of Thailand and the perfect fusion of tradition and innovation in every bite of its handcrafted, award-winning creative products.
